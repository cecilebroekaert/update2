Utopian environment

An environment/installation that I made in 2015-2016 while working on the theme 'utopia'.                         
The woolen balls are all knitted by hand. I wanted a lot of different colours and mixes of wool, because of the hectic and in the same way euphoric feeling you get from the colours.
The woolen balls should bring a sort of joy to environments that are ordinary, unimaginative or completly the opposite 'a dystopia'. By laying the woolen balls in a specific environment it changes not only the atmosphere of the environment but also the living space of the habitant or visitor.
