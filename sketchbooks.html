The brown sketchbook series

It all started with a tiny brown sketchbook and the urge to draw. I wanted to find pleasure and spontanity in drawing again cause I was losing the fun of it all due to stress, deadlines, my perfectionistic behavior and the constant criticism on my work by teachers, friends and even family members.I was a bit lost in it all.
This sketchbook "BB REEKS NUMMER 1" was made in the beginning of 2017. The sketches are made in pencil and show friends, family memebers and myself.
